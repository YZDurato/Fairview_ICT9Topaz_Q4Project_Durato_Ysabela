Anne Hathaway


About her

Born on November 12, 1982, meet Anne Jacqueline Hathaway. A worldwide-known American actress, known for her big-screen debut that came with the series "The Princess Diaries" in which she played the role of Mia Thermopolis back in 2001.

Movies / Series She Played

One of the roles she played and was known for was the White Queen from the movie Alice in Wonderland (2010)
